The music streaming platform's name I chose was abyss and hence wanted the landing page to have a dark with neon vibes. Hence opted for the Black with red as the primary colors with blue to add accent. Auto-layout was used in places needed, also components were made and reused wherever necessary. The featured section consist of custom made playlist cards and artist cards matching the theme of the platform's name. Future improvements include animated features section, more representation of features to be included.
